Realistic rally game...

Dirt Rally is a racing sports game for PC, PS4 and Xbox One developed by Codemasters. On launch the game featured 17 different cars and 36 stages from Monte Carlo, Powys and Argolis.

The game is the best rally game ever developed. In game, the player is accompanied by a realistic sounding co-pilot who helps you navigate the stages. The game’s physics are very realistic, one small mistake can cost you a lot of time.

Dirt Rally is a really good game, however it’s not everyone's cup of tea. I personally found the game to be too difficult without a proper set up. The game is nearly impossible to play with a mouse and keyboard, its very difficult to play with a gamepad and it is definitely designed for expensive racing wheel setups.
