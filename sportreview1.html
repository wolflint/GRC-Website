8 Ball Pool

Get ready to pool...

Woah!

Impressive!

A good pool game...

8 Ball Pool is a pool billiards sports game for Android, iOS and PC developed by Miniclip. The game has a currency system, through which the player can get upgraded cues. To gain coins, a player has to win a game. Different game modes have different buy-in prices, therefore some game modes are designed for more experienced players.

The game controls are quite easy to learn. To aim the cue, you drag your finger or mouse across the screen (this can also be customized to a tap or click instead of the drag) and you use the cue power bar to control the force.
