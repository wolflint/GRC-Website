A good Battlefield game...

Battlefield 1 is a first-person shooter game developed by EA DICE, it came out worldwide on October 21, 2016. Battlefield 1 is based on World War 1, and it does a brilliant job at conveying the savagery of the Great War. The prologue to the story mode focuses on how inevitable death was during WW1. If you die during the prologue you respawn as another person. This cycle repeats itself until the objective is completed.

During the story mode (“War Stories”) you can play through multiple tales which are organised in a non-linear anthology format, meaning that you can play them in any particular order. Each tale shows a different character's perspective. Each character has different views and motivations. These tales are quite interesting to play though.

The online aspect of the game is very similar to older Battlefield games. It consists of large maps, vehicles and loads of weapons and equipment customization. The game is quite cruel to beginner players, it is nearly impossible to stay alive long enough to get a kill.
