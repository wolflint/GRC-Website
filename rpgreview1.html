The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action role-playing video game developed and published by CD Projekt RED. This astonishing game was released worldwide for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One in 2015. The game is the third in the series, it is the final sequel to 'The Witcher' and 'The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings'. These games are based on the series of fantasy novels by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski.

The story follows the adventures of a ‘witcher’ (monster slayer) called Geralt of Rivia. The game consists of hundreds of main quests, secondary quests, monster contracts and treasure hunts. The game has multiple large open world maps which are connected via fast travel.

The game's massive open-world map and the large amount of quests add up to endless hours of gameplay. The epic story and well voice characters make this game a pleasure to play. The game's mechanics can be quite difficult to understand, however they make the gameplay difficult enough so that they game is interesting but not tedious. Overall, ‘Witcher 3: Wild Hunt’ is a great game.
