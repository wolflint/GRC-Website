A very realisting simulation game...

Reigns is a simulation card game for iOS, Android and PC developed by DevolverDigital. The player takes the role of the king, who has to make decisions in order to maintain balance in the kingdom.

During the game, the player receives requests from NPCs and has to choose whether to agree or disagree. Each decision can have a positive or negative impact on one of the four factions (church, people, army and merchants). These factions need to be balanced, getting one of them too low or too high will put an end to your reign.

This game is really fun to play, however it can be extremely cruel. My personal best so far is 56 years in power.
